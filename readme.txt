
what.it.do is a list-making tool to help you keep track of your favorite TV shows, movies, books, games, and music.

Catalog your collections, rate your favorites, keep track of your progress, and share your lists with your friends!

An example profile can be seen here:   < http://whatitdo.herokuapp.com/~oz >
